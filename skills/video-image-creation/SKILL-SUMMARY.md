## Color System

**Purposeful color usage:**

- **Green** = Positive, optimal, growth
- **Yellow/Gold** = Caution, attention, transition
- **Orange** = Warning, energy
- **Red** = Danger, decline, critical
- **Blue** = Trust, information, professional
- **Purple** = Premium, advanced
- **Gray** = Neutral, baseline

**Not random colors. Colors tell part of the story.**

---

## Text Hierarchy

**5-level system:**

1. **Main Headline** - LARGEST (60-80pt)
2. **Key Data/Numbers** - VERY LARGE (50-70pt)
3. **Subheadings** - Medium-Large (30-40pt)
4. **Labels/Annotations** - Medium (20-30pt)
5. **Source Citations** - Small (12-16pt)

**Amount rules:**
- Headline: 3-8 words
- Subheading: 4-10 words
- Labels: 1-3 words each
- Explanatory text: Under 15 words per section

**Let visuals do the talking.**
