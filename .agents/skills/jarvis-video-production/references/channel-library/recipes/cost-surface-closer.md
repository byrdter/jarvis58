# Recipe — Cost-Surface Closer

The shape for closing a part / segment. Promotes a foreshadow element (typically a token meter) from accent to subject, decomposes the cost, lands a punchline, then hands off to the next part with a chapter card.

## Proven in
- Module 09 — Context Window (closer of Part 1)

## Combines
- Inherited state from previous module (centerpiece + satellites + thin foreshadow meter)
- `cost-meter-stacked` (promotes from foreshadow to subject)
- `caption-ribbon` (left-aligned narrow variant)
- `chapter-card` (Part-N opener with progress dots)
- Satellites migrate from corners → horizontal row at bottom

## Beat structure

```
0–5s     Inherit previous module's end state exactly
5–13s    Hero element + satellites fade to invisible; meter promotes to wide + gains scale + header
13–30s   Always-on cost bands stack from bottom to top, synced to VO ("system / schemas / conversation / results")
30–46s   Sub-type contribution bands land on top, synced to VO punchlines ("Heavy / Compact / Barely / Result not history")
46–51s   Caption ribbon left-aligned punchline ("BEFORE YOU'VE DONE ANY REAL WORK.")
51–56s   Meter relaxes to thin right-edge bar; satellites re-materialize in horizontal row at bottom; eyebrow flips "PART 1 — COMPLETE." → "PART 2 — DEEP DIVES."
56–61s   Chapter card slides in: "NEXT — X. SIX SEGMENTS." with 6 progress dots
61–end   Hold + cut
```

## Energy curve
- Calmer than the arrival climax — this is the closer, not another peak.
- Steady lift through meter promotion + band stacking.
- Sustains through synthesis ribbon.
- Settles at the meter-relax + migration.
- Tiny lift at the chapter card for the Part-2 promise.

## Why this shape
The closer needs a DIFFERENT subject than the climax. If the climax was the arrival of Tools, the closer is "but every tool has a cost." Different subject demands different shape: telemetry / cost surface, not centerpiece-with-satellites.

The migration of satellites from corners to a row at the bottom is the narrative signal: "Part 1 is over, the table of contents for Part 2 is set."

## When NOT to use
- Climax modules (use arrival-module instead).
- Opening modules (use chapter-card-led opener).
- Mid-arc teaching beats (use parallel-set or single-shape).
