03 · Principles

Visual Principles

01
Edit, not decorate
Every overlay earns its place. If it doesn't carry meaning the voice is delivering at that moment, it doesn't ship.
02
Big and few
One primary thing per frame, large. Reading happens at 4 feet on a phone — small text dies. Hero text is 72–120px, body never below 28px.
03
Compose, don't bespoke
Same 16 components recombine across all videos. The Cloudflare frame and the JARVIS-architecture frame share parts. Polish lives in the library.
