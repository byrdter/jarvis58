# Video 12 Design System

## Style Prompt

Premium technical editorial video system for Byrddynasty agentic AI lessons. The frame should feel like a serious engineering investigation: dark ink canvas, precise browser and terminal mockups, large editorial serif claims, restrained amber highlights, and quiet motion that reveals evidence rather than decorating it.

## Colors

- Background: `#0B0E14`
- Surface: `#161A23`
- Elevated surface: `#1F2530`
- Primary text: `#E8ECEF`
- Secondary text: `#9AA3AD`
- Byrddynasty amber: `#F0B742`
- Success green: `#5EBE89`
- Warning red: `#E5605C`
- Link/code blue: `#5B9DF9`
- Border: `#2A3140`

## Typography

- Hero/editorial titles: Fraunces
- UI, labels, captions: Inter
- Code, URLs, terminal text: JetBrains Mono

## Motion

- Evidence enters first; numbers and conclusions arrive after the claim is established.
- Use short staggered reveals, light scale, and vertical/opacity transitions.
- Cursor/highlight motion should direct attention to source evidence, not perform unnecessary movement.

## What Not To Do

- Do not use generic node-and-line diagrams as the primary visual.
- Do not use default blue/purple tech gradients as the brand.
- Do not let captions or lower bars cover important source text.
- Do not create one-off scene styles when a reusable component can carry the shot.
