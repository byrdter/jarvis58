# Video 12 Middle Batch Proof

This proof tests the middle of Video 12 without side avatars. The goal is not a finished full video; it is a batch of distinct full-screen scene languages that can replace static panels.

## Rules

- Every segment must have a visible action arc: discover, collapse, transport, block, route, or assemble.
- No generic text panels as the main visual.
- Use short labels only where the viewer needs them.
- Cursor or pointer movement must trigger a change.
- Keep the screen full and active: big objects, readable numbers, moving routes, and focus changes.

## Palette

- Base: `#020611`, `#071424`
- Cyan: `#32f7ff`
- Red: `#ff3f66`
- Green: `#63ff85`
- Amber: `#ffb64a`
- Violet: `#9f72ff`
