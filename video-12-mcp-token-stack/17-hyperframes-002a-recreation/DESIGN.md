# 002A HyperFrames Recreation Design

## Style Prompt

Recreate the 002A still as a live HyperFrames scene: a premium engineering documentary frame showing multiple integrations feeding schema pages into a central AI context window before the user starts work. Match the original structure closely enough to test HyperFrames capability: large title, six tool boxes, central glowing window, empty prompt below, page streams, cyan energy paths, and slow inward page motion.

## Colors

- Background: `#050b16`
- Panel glass: `rgba(18, 39, 62, 0.72)`
- Cyan glow: `#4eeaff`
- Blue stream: `#1fb7ff`
- Purple edge: `#d466ff`
- Amber/GitHub: `#ffb22f`
- Red/Sentry/Splunk: `#c82d52`
- Green/Grafana: `#56e486`
- Jira blue: `#1687ff`

## Motion Rules

- Pages drift slowly toward the central context window for the full 10 seconds.
- Cyan streams pulse continuously and subtly, not with harsh cuts.
- Tool boxes and central window should feel like a stable hero frame; motion comes from pages, stream energy, glow, and slight camera drift.
- Keep labels minimal and readable. Do not recreate every tiny generated-image label.
